The admissions process in Charulata Pre-School is very transparent. Anyone is welcome to apply here without any reference. Absolutely no donations are accepted; only published fees are required.

It is During the admissions process, interviews are held by a panel of senior management, not a single individual. The interview panel is a team of qualified individuals from Charulata Pre-School's senior management. All decisions for admission are made through a committee, not by any one individual.

No external agents/agencies are appointed or involved in the admissions process; the Admissions Office is the only point of contact for all admissions-related inquiries
